About me

My name is Arvid Berlin, I am 30 years old and I was born and raised in Stockholm, Sweden. My biggest interest in life, besides designing websites of course, is film.

I grew up with films being a big part in my household, since both my mom and aunt work in the industry, and probably therefor quickly developed a love for it. But I also grew up, as probably a lot of boys do, as a tech nerdy guy with a big interest in computer games and such.

I've now worked in the film industry since 2020 (great year to start), but the inconsistency and job insecurity moved my sights towards different interests.
So here I am, starting a new career and loving the process of learning completely different skills in web development.
